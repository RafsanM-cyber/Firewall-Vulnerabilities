The World of Hardware

In the coming years, Hardware has become an increasing demand. From companies to businesses to governments, Hardware is becoming more widespread, and to maintain with this boom devices has to be widespread. Additionally, with the rise of AI and Quantum Computing in recent decades, this problem of scarcity only grows. With these devices requiring high power, it is to be known that these devices are very hard to mantain both on a physical and software level, and with other threats to consider like security flaws high quality devices become the only option for many businesses.

Introduction to Firewalls

Among the attraction of all the hardware, Firewalls is a big one to consider. If you don't know what a firewall is, it is basically a device that controls internet traffic to prevent unauthorized access of a certain network. Of course with a network secure, the security providing this network security has to be maitained, orelse attackers will breach into it. There are various problems with firewalls, and one specefically, used by a bunch of companies, Fortinet's FortiGate, a modern firewall that aims to provides comprehensive security over internet traffic over a network. This 'comprehensive security' will be covered more in the Product webpage.
